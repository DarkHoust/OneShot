OneShot is a top down Puzzle/Adventure game with unique gameplay capabilites. You, as the player, must guide Niko through a mysterious world, utilizing items, characters, and the enviroment to progress.

The Life of a lost child and the fate of a steadly decaying world hang in the balance... and not everyone is interested in preserving them.
